Instructions

Welcome to Nuclear Sweeper, a thrilling variation of the classic Minesweeper game with a radioactive twist!

Objective:

Your goal is to uncover all safe rooms on the board while marking radioactive rooms (mines) to prevent accidental exposure.

How to Play:
  • Sweep the Board: Click on the rooms to reveal whether they are safe or contain radioactive materials. Each room you uncover will either show a number indicating adjacent radioactive rooms or be empty.
  • Mark Radioactive Rooms: Use flags to mark rooms suspected of containing radioactive materials. This helps in avoiding these rooms and sealing them off.
  • Avoid Radioactive Rooms: Entering a room with radioactive material results in a loss. All remaining radioactive rooms will be revealed and the game ends.
  • Win the Game: Successfully mark all radioactive rooms and uncover all safe rooms to win.
